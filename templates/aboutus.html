About Us

Vybe transcends the ordinary, redefining what it means to be a music streaming service. It's not just a platform; it's your gateway to an immersive journey into the boundless realms of music. At Vybe, we're more than enthusiasts; we're believers in the transformative power of music.

Join us on this extraordinary musical odyssey. Let Vybe be the soundtrack to your memories, emotions, and experiences. Embrace the rhythm, feel the vibes, and let the music take you places you've never been.

Vybe isn't just a music streaming service; it's a lifestyle, a community, and an expression of the profound connection we share with music. Experience Vybe—where music becomes an integral part of your journey.

This music streaming application was developed as a part of project for IIT Madras Modern Application Development I course.
